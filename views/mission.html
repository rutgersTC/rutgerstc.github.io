Our Mission

Tzu-Chi's Mission:

Tzu-Chi's mission is to alleviate the suffering of the less fortunate and those who have fallen on hard times, through the principles of compassion and joyful giving. Focus is given primarily to eight "missions," known as the "Eight Footprints."

RUTC's Mission:

As a branch of Tzu-Chi, RUTC's chapter mission is to spread awareness of Tzu-Chi and its principles (including charity and environmental awareness) across campus and participate in local humanitarian efforts.
