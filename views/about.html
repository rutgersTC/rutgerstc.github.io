About Us

Who are we?

We are Rutgers Tzu-Ching (RUTC), Tzu-Chings of the chapter based in Rutgers University. Tzu-Chings represent the collegiate division of Tzu-Chi.

Tzu-Chi?

Tzu-Chi (慈濟) - which means "compassion relief" - is an international, charitable organization founded on Buddhist principles by Dharm Master Cheng-Yen in 1966.

Rutgers?

Our focus at Rutgers is to promote awareness about Tzu-Chi and its principles, and provide community service through food pantries and preparing relief packages for the less fortunate..
